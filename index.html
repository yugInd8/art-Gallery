The Sun to someone's Moon

What saved the shinobi world was no power, no ninshu, and neither some jutsu. It was friendship. All we sometimes need is a friend who always believes in us no matter what. But its selfish to want a someone to believe in you. You can never for sure expect someone to believe in you. But what you can assure is to be there for a friend even when they say they don't need you. Sometimes you should be the one who is the savior. Sometimes you have to be the Naruto to your Sasuke. Never abandon a true friend. Remember, "Those who break the rules are scum. But those who abandon their friends are worse than scum."
- Obito Uchiha.
